Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Authentication

We use Zoho OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication. You authorize our extension through Zoho's official login page - we never see or store your password.

What We Store

  • ‚úì Zoho Account ID: A unique identifier to link your refresh token.
  • ‚úì Refresh Token: Stored securely on our backend to fetch your count.
  • ‚úì Unread Count: Cached temporarily (60s) to minimize API calls.
  • ‚úì Mail Metadata: Sender, subject, and snippet (cached locally for 30s).

What We DO NOT Store

  • ‚úï Your email address, username, or passwords.
  • ‚úï Full email content, bodies, or attachments.
  • ‚úï Contact lists or any personally identifiable information.

Data Security

All communication uses HTTPS encryption. Refresh tokens are stored in an encrypted database. Our backend is hosted on Render.

Data Retention

Data is retained only while connected. Disconnect via Extension Settings or your Zoho Account dashboard.
